How to unlock your app

⏱ DaIntervalPro — step by step
  1. Open DaIntervalPro on your Garmin watch and press START to open the main menu.
  2. Scroll down and select Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Enter Pro Key.
  4. You will see 6 digit boxes. Use UP / DOWN to change each digit. Press START to advance to the next digit.
  5. After entering all 6 digits, press START on the last digit to submit. Your watch will vibrate twice and play a success tone — Pro is now unlocked. 🎉
  6. Note: your watch must have an active Bluetooth / Wi-Fi connection for the code to validate. If you see “Network error”, open the Garmin Connect app and try again.
⚡ FastFlow — step by step
  1. Open FastFlow on your Garmin watch and long-press UP to open the app menu.
  2. Select Settings then scroll down to Unlock Pro and tap it.
  3. You will see 6 digit boxes. Use UP / DOWN to change each digit. Press START to advance to the next digit.
  4. After the 6th digit, press START to submit. Your watch will vibrate twice and play a success tone. The main screen will update to show Pro status. 🎉
  5. Note: ensure Bluetooth is active and Garmin Connect is running on your phone before entering the code. If validation fails, check your connection and try again.

Common questions

How long until I receive my code?
Codes are emailed to your PayPal address within a few minutes of payment confirmation.
Can I use the code on multiple watches?
Each code is single-use and locks to the first device that redeems it. Contact support@dasecure.com if you upgrade your watch.
I entered the code correctly but got “Invalid code”
Double-check each digit and ensure there are no leading zeros missed. If the issue persists, email support@dasecure.com with your PayPal receipt.
I see “Network error” on my watch
Open Garmin Connect on your phone, wait for it to sync, then try entering the code again. The watch needs a live data connection to validate.
